# Grok Export Viewer Portable

**A beautiful and convenient offline viewer for a full Grok (xAI) export**

Open the entire `export_data` folder with one click and view your chats in an interface that closely resembles the original Grok experience — with support for tables, code, images, and attachments.

---

### Features

- Beautiful interface with message bubbles
- Search across all chats
- **Timeline** — filter messages by date and time
- Full Markdown support: tables, lists, code, headings
- Built-in image viewer
- Download any file with one click
- Import by selecting the `export_data` folder, or any parent folder
- Automatic detection of `prod-grok-backend.json`
- Full asset support: `prod-mc-asset-server/_/<uuid>/content`
- Export any chat to `.md`
- Fully offline, with no data being sent anywhere

---

### How to Get Your Export from Grok

1. Go to [grok.x.ai](https://grok.x.ai) or open the Grok app
2. Click your avatar → **Settings** → **Data Controls**
3. Click **Download your data** or **Export your data**
4. Confirm the request
5. Wait for the email and download the ZIP archive
6. Unzip the archive — inside, you’ll find the `export_data` folder

---

### How to Use

1. Download or simply open the **grok-viewer-i18n.html** file
2. Open it in your browser — **Chrome** or **Edge** is recommended
3. Click the large **“Select export_data folder”** button
4. Select the `export_data` folder, or the folder containing your user UUID inside it
5. Wait for it to load — all chats and files will appear automatically

(Optional)

6. Delete all your chats in Grok for privacy

(Just kidding.)

**Done.** You can now browse your entire Grok conversation history in a convenient format.

---

### Helpful Tips

- The closer the folder you select is to `prod-grok-backend.json`, the faster the export will load
- Images and files are automatically linked by UUID
- For extremely large exports, the first load may take 10–40 seconds
- Works fully offline and portably — nothing is sent anywhere, and nothing needs to be installed

---

**Made especially for those who want to preserve and comfortably read their Grok archive.**
